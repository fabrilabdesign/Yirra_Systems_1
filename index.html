Welcome to the AA3 Drone Project

The AA3 is a high-performance 8-inch drone, 3D-printed with carbon fiber nylon, designed for durability and repairability. It features a modular 6S battery system with Molicel P50B cells, an on-button with MOSFET power management, and is built to carry an O4 Pro air unit.

Drone Features

  • 8-inch drone with carbon fiber nylon body and TPU camera mount
  • Resin-printed top cover for sleek design
  • Modular 6S battery with Molicel P50B cells
  • On-button with MOSFET for efficient power management
  • Designed for O4 Pro air unit compatibility
  • Powered by iFlight E555 ESC and 2807 motors with 8-inch props
  • Partially open-source for easy repairs after crashes

About the Project

The AA3 Drone Project is a partially open-source initiative aimed at delivering a rugged, high-performance drone that can be 3D-printed and repaired by enthusiasts. The carbon fiber nylon frame, TPU camera mount, and resin top cover ensure durability, while the modular design allows for easy upgrades and repairs.
